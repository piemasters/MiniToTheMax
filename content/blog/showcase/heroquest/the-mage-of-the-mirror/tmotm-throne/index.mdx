## Miniature Review

This throne is much more ornate than the one in the core box set. I like the mix of the natural vines and the manmade
sculpted details. It might not be the comfiest thing to sit on but it is extremely regal.

## Roundup

I really like how the silver came out on this, it works well with the blue and gits the piece nicely. The red and blue
are almost opposite colors of the original throne so the two tie together nicely.
