## Miniature Review

This Weapon Rack is fairly similar to the one in the core box, but has much more ornate weaponry with gems, curved
blades and detailed trim. The stand is more chunky as well, so feels less fragile.

## Painting Techniques

### Wood

<Paint name={'Dryad Bark'} type={'air'} />
<Paint name={'Mournfang Brown'} type={'air'} />
<Paint name={'XV88'} type={'air'} />

I airbrushed a few different tones of brown, finishing with a brighter color than desired so the following wash
would both apply shadow to the wood grain and bring the overall brightness down to the desired finish.
Finally I gave it a light drybrush to help further emphasis the grain of the wood.

## Roundup

I went with a new color for the shield to help it stand out and add some additional color to the piece. There is
maybe a bit too much gold on both the stand and the weaponry, perhaps using bronze on the stand would help the
weapons pop more.
