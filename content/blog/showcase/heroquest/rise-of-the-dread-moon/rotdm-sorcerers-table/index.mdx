## Miniature Review

The Sorcerers Table has a similar look to the one in the core box with the large book in the center, but has a few
different details. Rather than candles and a sculpted top it instead has a cloth covering with a skull and crystal ball.
It's not a lot of detail but it looks good and is obvious when playing what it is and the two work well as a pair.

## Roundup

The green really helps make the book pop, and again the transfers look great as a replacement for my terrible
squiggly lines. The crystal ball also came out pretty well, I stippled on the white so it's not fully smooth but
instead gives it a cloudy look.
