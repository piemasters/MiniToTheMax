> While all Gnomes possess an ability to conjure minor illusions, some are more gifted than others. Such Gnomes are
> known as illusionists, and claim to have been blessed with the gifts by Ringil himself (that's the Gnomish god of
> trickery, if you didn't know - Ed). Of course, with trickery being held in high regard, illusionists are very much
> at the forefront of Gnomish society and will often be seen at Blood Bowl games, delighting the fans with
> spectacular displays of magic!

## Miniature Review

The Illusionist is a great model, and I love the pose, holding out an illusionary ball with the real ball behind their
back. The alternate sculpt has the balls the other way around which is a nice touch. The model is full of character and
the different shaped hat is great to help make her stand out more from the the rest of the team.
